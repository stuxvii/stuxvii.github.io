Requirements:
around 20 gbs of space
QEMU
A disk setup image of Mac OSX
If you've already installed QEMU, scroll all the way to the bottom.
If not, follow these instructions to get QEMU on your windows machine:

Download and install MSYS2.
When installed, open UCRT64.
After opening UCRT64, you will see a terminal on your screen, this is were you will get QEMU. to do so, type and send the following command:
pacman -S mingw-w64-ucrt-x86_64-qemu
Once successful, you may now prepare the VM.
