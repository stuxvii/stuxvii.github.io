Guide

Download PCK Bruteforcer & gdsdecomp
Download the .pck and .wasm files

* Compile PCK Bruteforcer

PCK Bruteforcer doesn't have any available pre-built binaries, so you will have to compile this yourself to get this up and running.
Open Visual Studio
Click "Clone a repository"
Enter "https://github.com/DmitriySalnikov/GodotPCKExplorer.git" in the repository location section and hit "Clone"
When it finishes cloning, double click "GodotPCKExplorer.sln", open the Bruteforcer directory, double click the PCKBruteforcer.UI file, and finally, hit the CTRL+F5 keys to build and run.
Bruteforce the encryption key with PCK Bruteforcer, place the .wasm in the .exe field (yes, because .wasm are basically .exe files for web browsers) the .pck in the .pck field and hit "try to get the key"; After a while it'll give you the key for that build.

Good job! Now open gdsdecomp, click the RE Tools button, set the key to the one PCK Bruteforcer gave you, and export the .pck into a neatly organized folder.
